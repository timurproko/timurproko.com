Overview · R&D Phase

AI-driven face & voice

AI-driven facial animation and voice synthesis produce lifelike facial expressions and human-like speech — the foundation for believable avatars and immersive interaction.

  • Facial recognition & 3D modeling
  • Emotion analysis for expression
  • Text-to-speech voice synthesis

Applied across gaming, entertainment, virtual reality and voice assistants.

Process · Face Reconstruction

From photos to a 3D face

Image-to-3D reconstruction turns 2D photographs and video into detailed, accurate 3D representations of a human face.

  • Multi-view capture to dense point cloud
  • Lifelike 3D facial models from 2D source
  • Feeds graphics, VR and medical imaging
Process · Mesh Retopology

High-poly to clean base mesh

Retopology rebuilds a dense scan as a simplified, optimized base mesh — clean edge flow that is efficient and ready for animation.

  • Simplified, optimized topology
  • Deformation-friendly edge flow
  • Detail projected back onto the base
Retopo Base
Process · Character Design

Texturing & realistic render

Lifelike textures are authored and applied to the model, then rendered to achieve a high level of realism.

  • Skin, hair, makeup & garment materials
  • Physically based texturing & lighting
  • Rendering tuned for believable realism
